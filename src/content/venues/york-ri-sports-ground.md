The club is based at York RI Sports Ground, New Lane, Holgate, York, North Yorkshire. This is a suburb of York about one mile west of the city centre.

We enjoy the facilities of the Clubhouse and Sports Ground where the club practices outdoor target archery and clout, using recurve, compound and longbows.
