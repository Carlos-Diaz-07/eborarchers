title: "Venues & Times - Ebor Archers of York"
description: "Find out where and when Ebor Archers shoot. Indoor and outdoor venues across York with shooting schedules."
outdoor_heading: "April to September"
outdoor_text: |
  During the outdoor season we shoot at York RI Sports Ground, New Lane, Holgate, York. Our regular shooting times are Wednesday and Friday evenings, normally commencing approximately 6:30 pm depending on the light levels, and we shoot through to dusk.

  We also have use of the field at other times via a separate arrangement.
indoor_heading: "October to March"
indoor_text: |
  During the indoor season we shoot on a Monday evening in the Sports Hall of Askham Bryan College. We also have use of Acomb Methodist Church Hall on Friday evenings. Sessions assemble at 6:50 pm at both venues.
