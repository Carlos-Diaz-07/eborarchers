I have received the paperwork for this years Selby Summer Postal League and we have been seeded into Division 4. The matches will take place one each month from May to September and our team will be selected from the highest 3 scores shot each month. The round to be shot is a Short Metric, so get your sight marks of 50 and 30 m sorted early ready for the first match in May. Our target score for the division is 1700.
