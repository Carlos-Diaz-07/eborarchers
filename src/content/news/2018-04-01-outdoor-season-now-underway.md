Well that's the end of another indoor season and we are outdoors from now until mid September. Shooting nights are Tuesday and Thursday at Long Marston. As it currently gets dark quite early in the evening there will be no fixed start time until mid May, so please arrive as soon as you can, say between 5.30 and 6.00pm, and we will shoot until dusk.
