The Yorkshire Clout Weekend was hosted by the Archers of East Riding at Hutton Cranswick, on behalf of the Yorkshire Archer Association. Geoff Ingleby and myself were amongst the medals. The weather was against us all weekend with strong variable cross winds on the Saturday for the World Archery Round and a breeze and rain for the Yorkshire Championships on the Sunday.

Despite the conditions Geoff came 2nd in the Barebow class on Saturday and 2nd in the Yorkshire Championships on Sunday. Whilst I was 2nd in the Recurve class on Saturday and 3rd in the Yorkshire Championships on the Sunday, along with winning the Best Gentlemen's Clout Award.
