John Ross made a great start to his indoor season with a win in the Barebow class at the Archers of Bridlington & Burton Agnes Portsmouth Tournament on 6th November 2022. This was his first open tournament and secured his success with a score of 512.

Also at the tournament were David O'Connor, Steve Prinn and Ray Chaplin shooting in the Recurve class. They scored 562, 560 and 518 respectively, but this added up to a Team score of 1640 which was enough for them to secure the Team Champions on the day.
