Last night at the Long Marston Village Hall AGM the constitution of the 250 Prize Draw was changed to omit the drawing of a bottle of Whisky as it no longer has the effect of enticing usage of the Hall as it once did. So for next year and thereafter there will be no Whisky draw at the monthly meeting. However, we were still in the current period so there was one more draw to do and guess what to the delight of the Villagers Ebor Archers won the bottle of Whisky again!!!!, a fitting end to the arrangement.

I will as usual raffle off the bottle of whisky at 1 pound per ticket, so let me know how many you want.

I will be contacting all existing ticket holders to collect or otherwise your fees for next year's subs for the prize draw in due course.
