On 17 September 2021 Ebor Archers concluded its outdoor season at York RI Sports Ground and will return there in the Spring of 2022.

On 20 September 2021 Ebor Archers started indoor shooting at Askham Bryan College and will continue each Monday evening until the end of March 2022.

On 24 September 2021 Ebor Archers started indoor shooting in Acomb Methodist Church Hall, Front Street Acomb, York and will continue each Friday evening until the end of March 2022.
