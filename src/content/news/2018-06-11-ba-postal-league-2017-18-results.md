The results are now published for the BA Postal League for the winter of 2017/18 in which we entered a team in the Portsmouth Recurve Bow category. We were placed in Division 12 where we had mixed fortunes with 2 wins and 3 defeats, but this was sufficient to place us 3rd in the division. The scoring was very close between the top three clubs with aggregate scores over all the matches of 8245 and 8277 against our 8206, so you can see we were not far off the mark.

I have not had the results of the first round of the summer postal league yet, but I will pop a note up when it arrives.
