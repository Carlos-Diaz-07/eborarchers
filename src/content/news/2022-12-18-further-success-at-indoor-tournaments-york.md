David O'Connor and Ray Chaplin attended York Archers Society Double Portsmouth Tournament on 20th November 2022. This was held at Sandburn Hall and without the heating system it was a chilly event. Nevertheless they overcame the elements finishing in second and third places, respectively, in the Recurve class with scores of 1125 and 1099.
