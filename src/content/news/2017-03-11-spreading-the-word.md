Now that our website is up and running, it would be great if you could help us to spread the word about it. One of the ways that you can do this is via social media on this 'News' page. If you have a Twitter or Facebook account, for example, you could 'share' a post /photo on your Twitter feed or 'like' one on your Facebook timeline. (To do this, you just need to click on the share icon at the bottom right of a post, and then click on the relevant social media icon).

Or alternatively just click the heart symbol to 'like' a post on this 'News' page... or add a comment where it's available (e.g. on 'Pete's Robin Hood' post).

The more people that do this, the quicker the word will spread.

Thanks!
