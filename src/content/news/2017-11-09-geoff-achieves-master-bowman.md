Congratulations to Geoff Ingleby who has achieve Master Bowman classification at Barebow Clout. This is a fantastic accolade given that Geoff converted from right to left handed a couple of years ago and then took up Barebow 2 seasons ago. This is the second year in a row he has managed this award and he crowned off a fantastic year by coming third in the National Clout.
