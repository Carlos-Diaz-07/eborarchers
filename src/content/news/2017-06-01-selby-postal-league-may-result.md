Well the outdoor season has started and the first round of the Selby Postal League has been shot. Unfortunately, we have not got off to a good start with a score of 1576 for the Short Metric round, shot by Steve Prinn, Pete Todman and myself, against Royston Heath 'A' who scored 1604. Lets hope for better conditions for next months round.
