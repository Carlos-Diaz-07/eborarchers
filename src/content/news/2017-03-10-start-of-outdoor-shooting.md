We will be starting outdoor shooting on Tuesday 4th April 2017 at Long Marston. On that evening we will assemble at Askham Bryan College at 5.45pm to transport the equipment back to Long Marston. Thereafter we will shoot on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at Long Marston. The start time during April, given the limited available light, will be dependant on how early members can arrive. But from May onwards I suggest we formalise the start time as 6.30pm so we all arrive together to set out the field.
