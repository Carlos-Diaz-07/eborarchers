A big congratulations to Geoff Ingleby who travelled down to the Bowmen of Rutland on Sunday 21st October to take part in a Double Metric Clout Competition. Shooting in the Barebow class he won the event with a score of 292, this giving him the third score he needed to achieve Grand Master Bowman classification for this year. It is quite sometime since the club has had a Grand Master Bowmen as part of our membership and it is a fitting reward for all the hard work and dedication Geoff has put in to change from being a right to left handed archer. He just needs to wait for the badge to drop through the letterbox so he can wear it with pride.

Many congratulations from all at Ebor Archers.
